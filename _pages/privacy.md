## Who we are

## What this policy covers

This policy explains how we handle personal data when you visit and-e.tech. It applies to visitors in the UK and EEA.

## Data we collect

### When you browse the site

Our host (**GitHub Pages**) may process technical data such as IP address, browser type, and requested pages in server logs. We do not control GitHub’s logging directly; see [GitHub’s privacy statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement).

### Analytics (only with your consent)

If you click **Accept** on the cookie banner, we load **Google Analytics 4** (Google Ireland Ltd.) to understand how the site is used (e.g. pages viewed, approximate location, device type). We do **not** load analytics if you reject or ignore the banner.

Google may process data in the US and other countries. Google’s privacy information: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Fonts and icons

We load **Font Awesome** styles from jsDelivr (a CDN). That request may disclose your IP address to the CDN. We treat this as necessary to display the site’s icons.

## Cookies

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|--------|---------|----------|------|
| `cookie-consent` | Stores your analytics choice (`accepted` or `rejected`) | 30 days | Strictly necessary |

Google Analytics sets additional cookies only after you accept. You can withdraw consent anytime via **Cookie settings** in the footer.

## Legal basis (UK GDPR)

- **Consent** — for analytics cookies and Google Analytics processing.
- **Legitimate interests** — for essential site operation, security, and hosting logs (balanced against your rights).

## How long we keep data

- Cookie preference: 30 days (then the banner appears again).
- Analytics: according to our Google Analytics retention settings (see Google Admin).

## Your rights

You may have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to processing, and to data portability where applicable. You may also lodge a complaint with the [ICO](https://ico.org.uk/) (UK).

To exercise rights relating to analytics data, you can also use [Google’s opt-out tools](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout) or clear cookies and choose **Reject** on this site.
